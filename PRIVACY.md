# Privacy Policy for Roll It

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by **Roll It** ("Stop scrolling. Start watching.") and how we use it.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**Roll It** is designed to operate as a local-first application on your iPhone:

* **No User Accounts**: You do not need to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information (such as name, email, or phone number) to use the app.
* **Local SwiftData Storage**: All your quiz preferences, watch logs, watch provider configurations, and movie skipping metrics are stored strictly on your device using Apple's local **SwiftData** framework. This data never leaves your device.
* **No Analytics or Tracking**: We do not include any tracking software, advertisements, or third-party analytics libraries that monitor your usage behavior or device information.

## 2. API & Network Requests

To discover and suggest movies, **Roll It** makes client-side queries to **The Movie Database (TMDB)** APIs.
* These queries are anonymous and do not contain any personal data or persistent user identifiers.
* To read more about how TMDB handles data, you can consult [TMDB's Privacy Policy](https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy).

## 3. Third-Party Links

The application may display watch provider links or redirect you to streaming platforms. We do not have control over, and are not responsible for, the content or privacy policies of those third-party services.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository page.
